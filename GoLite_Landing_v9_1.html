THE REAL COST OF TRAVEL TODAY

Every trip has five moments
that exhaust you.

01

Getting to the airport

You wrestle 3 heavy bags into an Uber that barely fits them. The driver is irritated. You arrive sweating, already exhausted. With kids or elderly parents? Add 30 minutes and twice the stress.

02

The check-in queue

You stand in a 40-minute queue. The agent says your bag is 3 kg overweight. That's ₹1,500 you didn't plan for. You repack on the airport floor. Your flight is in 90 minutes.

03

Managing kids & seniors through terminals

Your mother needs a wheelchair. Your 4-year-old is running the wrong way. You have two trolleys, a pram, and one pair of hands. This isn't a holiday — it's a military operation.

04

Waiting at the baggage belt

You've landed. You're tired. Now stand at a carousel for another 35 minutes watching bags go round — hoping yours isn't last, or worse, lost.

05

The exit from the airport

Get bags into a cab, across the city, up the stairs. Your destination wedding starts in 4 hours. You arrive at the hotel out of breath. This was supposed to be a celebration.

Burden builds across the journey. Every handoff adds one more layer of travel friction.
